# Lab

The lab holds the repository's research controls and retained test records.

- [`governance/`](governance/) — doctrine, rights, reproducibility, and architecture
- [`schemas/`](schemas/) — machine-readable contracts
- [`experiments/`](experiments/README.md) — falsifiable plans and accepted/rejected status
- [`evidence/`](evidence/README.md) — manifests, observations, and small lawful records
- [`examples/`](examples/README.md) — fictional records that exercise the schemas

Examples demonstrate structure; evidence records describe actual retained
artifacts. Neither becomes a physical conclusion without an explicit claim and
validation path.
